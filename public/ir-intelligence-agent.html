IR Intelligence Agent

Real-time analyst intelligence for IR officers during earnings calls and private meetings

OE Podium · Mithun & Devin · April 2026

IR officers fly blind into Q&A

Every quarter, IR officers at the world's largest financial institutions walk into earnings call Q&A with ten analysts raising their hands. They know names and firms. That's it.

They don't know what each analyst asked last quarter, what they pressed competitors on, or whether their sentiment is shifting. For companies where a single misstep moves the stock price, this is an unacceptable gap.

Three layers of data nobody else can assemble

PUBLIC

Public Earnings Transcripts

Every major company's earnings call transcripts are freely available. The agent ingests them all, building a complete picture of what any analyst has asked anyone, anywhere.

Available to competitors
